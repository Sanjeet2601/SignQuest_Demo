Welcome to SignQuest

SignQuest is a premier competition at NeurIPS 2026 dedicated to advancing research in Sign Language (SL) processing. With over 63 million Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing individuals in India, there is an urgent need for inclusive AI technologies that can bridge the communication gap between sign language users and the rest of society.

This competition introduces three core tasks, each with two tracks (RGB Video-based and Pose-based), resulting in six challenging tracks. By supporting both rich visual input and compact privacy-preserving skeletal representations, SignQuest enables systematic exploration of the trade-offs in multimodal sign language modeling.

Our goal is to drive innovation in spatio-temporal modeling, low-resource multimodal learning, and gloss-free sign language understanding — ultimately creating real-world assistive technologies for the DHH community.

Competition Tasks

1. Sign Language Translation (ISL → English)

Generate natural English sentences from continuous Indian Sign Language videos or pose sequences (gloss-free).

English Translation: "The ban would mean she can't compete in any national or other domestic events"

Tracks: Video-to-Text • Pose-to-Text

Evaluation: SacreBLEU (primary), ROUGE-L, chrF

Dataset & Starter Kit (Coming Soon)

2. Word / Gloss Recognition

Classify isolated signs from short video clips or pose sequences into correct word/gloss labels.

Label: National

Tracks: Video-based Recognition • Pose-based Recognition

Evaluation: Top-1 Accuracy (primary), Top-5 Accuracy

Dataset & Starter Kit (Coming Soon)

3. Word Presence Prediction

Given a continuous sentence video/pose and a query word, predict whether the word appears in the signed sentence.

Query Word: National
Sentence Video
Contains: National → Present

Tracks: Video-based • Pose-based

Evaluation: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score

Dataset & Starter Kit (Coming Soon)
